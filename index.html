About The Band

"SAMURAI" is a chrome-rock band founded by Johnny Silverhand and Kerry Eurodyne. The band started out playing in small bars and clubs, but rose to fame with their chart topping single "Blistering Love". They have 2 albums available, "Blistering Love" and their new album "Chippin' In" which you can buy now! above.
